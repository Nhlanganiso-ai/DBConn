Executive Summary
1.1 Business Overview

Abanyosi, located in Mapheleni, Ward 13, Mtubatuba, KwaZulu Natal, is poised to become a leading provider of high-quality eggs in the KwaZulu Natal province, specifically from our base in Mapheleni. Our business revolves around the rearing of laying hens, with a primary focus on delivering fresh eggs to our customers. We operate through physical distribution channels, ensuring that our products are accessible to a wide range of consumers. Our primary target market includes local restaurants, hotels, event caterers, culinary schools, and households in eMpangeni and the surrounding areas. With a dedicated team of 3 employees, we are committed to fostering strong relationships within the community and providing exceptional service to our clientele.
